A TV, or television, is an electronic device used for displaying moving visual media, such as broadcast programming, movies, or video games. It consists of a display screen, speakers, and various input/output connections. TVs can be either traditional cathode-ray tube (CRT) models or modern flat-panel displays, such as LCD or OLED screens. They receive signals through cable, satellite, or digital terrestrial broadcast and can also connect to other devices like DVD players, gaming consoles, or streaming services. TVs are a popular source of entertainment, information, and news in homes and public spaces.
