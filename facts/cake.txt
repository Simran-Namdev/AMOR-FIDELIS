A cake is a baked, sweet dessert that is typically made from a flour-based batter, sweetened with sugar or honey, and often containing additional ingredients like eggs, butter, milk, and flavorings. Cakes can be simple or elaborately decorated and can be served on various occasions, such as birthdays, weddings, and celebrations. They come in a wide range of flavors, from classic vanilla and chocolate to more exotic combinations like red velvet or carrot cake. Cakes are a beloved treat that brings people together and are a staple in many culinary traditions around the world.
