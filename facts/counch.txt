A couch, also known as a sofa, is a piece of upholstered furniture designed for seating multiple people. It typically consists of a raised seat, a backrest, and armrests, and is often used in living rooms, family rooms, or lounges. Couches can be made from various materials, including fabric, leather, or microfiber, and come in a variety of styles and sizes to suit different spaces and preferences. They provide a comfortable place for relaxation, socializing, or lounging and are a staple piece of furniture in many homes and public spaces.
