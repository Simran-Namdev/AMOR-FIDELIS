A laptop, also known as a notebook computer, is a portable, personal computer designed for mobile use. It combines a keyboard, display screen, and various input/output devices into a single, compact unit. Laptops are powered by batteries or an AC adapter and can be used for a variety of tasks, including word processing, web browsing, multimedia playback, gaming, and specialized applications. They offer portability and convenience, allowing users to work or engage in digital activities from virtually anywhere.
