Pizza is a savory dish consisting of a flat, round base made of dough, topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients like vegetables, meats, or herbs. It originated in Italy but has become a popular and widely adapted dish worldwide. Pizzas can be customized with a variety of toppings to suit individual tastes and preferences. They are typically baked in an oven and can be served as a main course or as an appetizer. Pizza is a beloved food that brings people together and is enjoyed in both casual and formal settings.
