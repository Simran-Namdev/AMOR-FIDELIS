A long, orange root vegetable with a crunchy texture and sweet flavor. Carrots are rich in beta-carotene, vitamins, and fiber, making them a healthy and versatile ingredient. They can be eaten raw, sliced, or grated into salads, coleslaws, and sandwiches. Carrots can also be cooked by boiling, steaming, roasting, or stir-frying and are used in soups, stews, and side dishes.
